Jeroo
Website: https://jeroo.org/beta/dashboard

Jeroo was the first major project that I ever worked on. With a group of three other team members, Jeroo was my capstone project during my senior year in college. The goal of the project was to rebuild and rethink an old program called 'Jeroo', moving it onto the internet with the main goal of accessibility and backwards compatibility. Jeroo is a programming education tool built with Angular 7, TypeScript, and OCaml.

This project taught me lessons on working with clients, working with a team, agile principles, meeting deadlines, and working with legacy code.
